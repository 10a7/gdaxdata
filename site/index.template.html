GDAX Ethereum Historical Data

This website contains downloadable historical data from the GDAX cryptocurrency exchange in pairs ETH/USD, ETH/BTC, and ETH/EUR. It is collected from the GDAX websocket feed with this listener script and then processed into monthly archives for download. You may find this data useful for algorithmic Ethereum trading strategies on the GDAX platform, or for other data analysis regarding the GDAX exchange. Data collected in these archives is released in the public domain (CC0 license).
This data is not checked for accuracy, contains gaps, and carries no warranty. Use at your own risk.
